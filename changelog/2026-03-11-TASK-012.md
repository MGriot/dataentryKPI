## Timestamp

## The "Why"
As the system moves toward dynamic, inter-dependent KPI calculations, the risk of circular dependencies (where KPI A depends on KPI B, which depends back on KPI A) becomes significant. Circularities would cause infinite loops or stack overflows during evaluation. This validation layer ensures system stability by detecting and preventing such cycles before they are saved to the database.

## Verification
- **Internal Cycle Detection**: Added `has_cycle` to `KpiDAG` to detect loops within a single visual formula.
- **Global Cycle Detection**: Implemented `check_for_global_circular_dependencies` to identify dependencies across multiple KPIs and target numbers.
- **Testing**: Created and ran `test_cycle_detection.py` which confirmed that both self-referencing nodes and multi-KPI dependency chains are correctly identified as cycles.
