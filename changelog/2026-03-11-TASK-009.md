## The "Why"
To improve the usability of the Data Entry page, the flat list of KPIs was replaced with a hierarchical organization. KPIs are now grouped by their respective Group and Subgroup, with collapsible sections for each level. This reduces visual clutter and allows users to quickly navigate to the relevant KPIs they need to update.

- **UI Structure**: Verified that the UI now renders nested frames: Group -> Subgroup -> KPI Input Boxes.
- **Navigation**: Confirmed that sections can be expanded/collapsed to manage canvas space.
