## Task ID & Title
TASK-014: Architecture: Standardized KPI Formulas & Centralized Distribution

## Timestamp
[15:30:00]

## The "Why"
Managing calculation formulas and distribution profiles at the individual target level (plant by plant, year by year) was redundant and prone to errors. By moving these settings to the centralized KPI definition level, we ensure that a KPI's logic remains consistent across the entire organization while simplifying the data entry process.

## Verification
- **Schema**: Added `is_calculated`, `formula_json`, and `default_distribution_profile` to the `kpis` table.
- **Backend**: Updated `annual.py` and `repartition.py` to prioritize these standardized definitions.
- **Dependency**: Implemented automated dependency extraction from the Node DAG, eliminating manual input definitions.
